Import Order Guide

For best results, import data in this order:

  1. Material Suppliers - Must exist before materials
  2. Materials - Raw materials (PP, ABS, etc.)
  3. Masterbatches - Colour/additive concentrates
  4. Customers - Customer information
  5. Categories - Item categories
  6. Locations - Warehouse locations
  7. Machines - Injection moulding machines
  8. Moulds - Requires customers (optional link)
  9. Items/Parts - Requires customers, materials, masterbatches, moulds
Tip: Use codes (customer_code, material_code, etc.) to link records together. The system will automatically look up and link the records.
