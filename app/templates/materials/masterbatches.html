What are Masterbatches?

Masterbatches are concentrated pigments or additives that are mixed with base polymer during injection moulding. They typically make up 1-4% of the total material weight.

Cost Calculation

When a part uses a masterbatch, the material cost is calculated as:
(Base material × (100-ratio%)) + (Masterbatch × ratio%)
